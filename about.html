About AutoFireExtinguishers.com

Our Mission

AutoFireExtinguishers.com exists to help vehicle owners make informed decisions about fire safety. Whether you drive a daily commuter, a weekend race car, a cross-country RV, or a work truck, having the right fire extinguisher — properly rated and properly mounted — can save lives and property.

We research fire extinguisher specifications, UL ratings, NFPA standards, and sanctioning body requirements so you don't have to dig through technical documents. Our goal is to present clear, accurate, and actionable information that helps you choose the right fire suppression equipment for your specific vehicle and use case.

How We Research

Our recommendations are based on:

  • Published manufacturer specifications and UL/FM ratings
  • NFPA standards (including NFPA 1192 for RVs and NFPA 10 for portable extinguishers)
  • Sanctioning body requirements (SFI, FIA, SCCA, NASA, NHRA)
  • FMCSA regulations for commercial vehicles
  • Aggregated owner reviews and feedback from automotive and motorsport communities

When we reference specifications, ratings, or certifications, we are citing manufacturer-published data and third-party certification bodies.
